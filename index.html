What is KJDG?


The Kristu Jayanti Developers' Group (KJDG) is the ultimate hub for students passionate about tech. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced coder, there's a place for you in our community. Learn, grow, and have fun while working on exciting real-world projects, hackathons, and skill-building sessions

We offer you more than just learning – we offer a platform to develop cutting-edge skills while making lifelong friends.Come and unlock your potential with us!

Why Join Us?


At KJDG, you'll experience hands-on learning, real-world projects, and mentorship from industry pros. Whether you're new or experienced, we’re here to help you grow.

  • 500+ active members and growing! Be part of a thriving tech community.
  • Successful launch of student-built apps and websites. Build products that make an impact.
  • Engaging sessions with experienced mentors. Learn from the best in the field.
  • Collaborate on projects to build your portfolio. Work on real projects that boost your resume.
  • Boost your soft skills. Improve teamwork, communication, and leadership.
